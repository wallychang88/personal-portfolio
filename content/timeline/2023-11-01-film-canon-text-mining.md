For Text Mining History and Literature, Casey Kaufman and I turned every Academy Award Best Original Screenplay nominee since 1940 into data — subtitles standing in for scripts. Most of the work was cleaning. The surprise: a model could tell winners from losers more readily than it could tell the decades apart.
