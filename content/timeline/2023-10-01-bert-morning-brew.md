An independent-research project under Prof. Matthew Wilkens: I scraped a year of the New York Times' morning newsletter and Morning Brew by hand. A classifier could tell the two apart at ~89% — but predicting the day a newsletter went out, by topic, by token, and by fine-tuned BERT, never worked at all.
