# Audit — round 3 design pages (2026-05-21)

Visual audit of the five round-3 pages on the Claude Design canvas
(`c31df236`), read against `WALLY.md` (canonical bio) and `design-brief.md`
(voice/visual brief). Read this end-to-end and react in the **Wally's
calls** section at the bottom — those notes feed directly into the next
Claude Design prompt OR into the Next.js port.

The design system itself (Bento + Atlas hybrid, 5-category ornament
library, type ramp, palette, motion grammar) reads as **locked**. What
follows is mostly **copy and date drift** that compounded across pages
because every page invented details from the same blanks in `WALLY.md`.

---

## TL;DR

**What's working** — voice register is mostly on-brand; ornament library
shows up correctly per category; trophy ordering and hero typography are
in the right place; the Bento + Atlas hybrid genuinely reads as a working
notebook, not a resume.

**What's broken** — three classes of issue:

1. **Date drift.** RodSmith dated 2023 (it's fall 2025). Extend dated 2024
   (it's May 2025). AmEx research dated 2023 (it's Feb–May 2024). Cornell
   Sprint Football tagged 2 seasons (it's 3, '21–'24). These mistakes
   repeat across `a-home`, `d-writing`, and `e-about`.
2. **Identity error on /about/.** Extend is described as fraud /
   payments / recovery. It's extended warranties — pricing & risk models
   for Peloton, Oura, Sonos, Michaels, Advance Auto Parts. This is the
   single highest-impact fix in the set.
3. **Invented anecdotes that look like ground truth.** Brook trout on
   elk-hair caddis, "glass vestibule + buzzer + guitar," Sharpie
   smudging at Tioga Pass, "I grew up in two states." All plausible,
   none in `WALLY.md`. You either confirm them or replace them.

The design system is good enough that **the next move is a copy-and-date
fix pass**, not a redesign. Once the copy is right, port to Next.js.
